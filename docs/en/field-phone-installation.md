# Telebirr Phone Setup - Simple Step-by-Step Guide

Use this guide from the first **Welcome** screen on the phone.

## Get these items from the supervisor

1. Get the Wi-Fi name and password.
2. Get the AirDroid QR code.
3. Get the OpenClaw setup code or QR code.
4. Get the Device Gateway URL.
5. Get the Telebirr Agent activation code.
6. Get the SIM 1 ICCID.
7. Get the SIM 2 ICCID.
8. Keep the Telebirr PIN for SIM 1 with you.
9. Keep the Telebirr PIN for SIM 2 with you.

<!-- pagebreak -->

## 1. Put in the two SIM cards

1. Hold the power button.
2. Tap **Power off**.
3. Remove the SIM tray.
4. Put the first Telebirr SIM into **SIM 1**.
5. Put the second Telebirr SIM into **SIM 2**.
6. Put the SIM tray back into the phone.
7. Hold the power button to turn on the phone.
8. Wait for the **Welcome** screen.

## 2. Start AirDroid setup

1. Stay on the first **Welcome** screen.
2. Tap the empty white area six times quickly.
3. Wait for the QR scanner to open.
4. Tap the Wi-Fi network name supplied by the supervisor.
5. Type the Wi-Fi password.
6. Tap **Connect**.
7. Point the camera at the AirDroid QR code.
8. Wait for the QR code to scan.
9. Tap **Continue**.
10. Tap **Next**.
11. Tap **Accept**.
12. Tap **Allow** when Android asks for permission.
13. Wait until the Android home screen appears.

<!-- pagebreak -->

## 3. Finish Biz Daemon setup

1. Open **Biz Daemon**.
2. Tap the first permission that is not enabled.
3. Tap **Allow**.
4. Return to **Biz Daemon**.
5. Tap the next permission that is not enabled.
6. Tap **Allow**.
7. Repeat until all basic permissions are enabled.
8. Tap **Accessibility**.
9. Tap **Biz Daemon**.
10. Turn on **Use Biz Daemon**.
11. Tap **Allow**.
12. Return to **Biz Daemon**.
13. Tap **Usage access**.
14. Tap **Biz Daemon**.
15. Turn on **Permit usage access**.
16. Return to **Biz Daemon**.
17. Tap **Install unknown apps**.
18. Turn on **Allow from this source**.
19. Return to **Biz Daemon**.
20. Tap **Notifications**.
21. Turn on **Allow notifications**.
22. Return to **Biz Daemon**.
23. Tap **Battery optimization**.
24. Tap **Allow** or **Don't optimize**.
25. Return to **Biz Daemon**.
26. Tap **Continue**.

<!-- pagebreak -->

## 4. Wait for the two applications

1. Keep the phone connected to Wi-Fi.
2. Keep the phone connected to the charger.
3. Wait for the **OpenClaw** icon to appear.
4. Wait for the **Telebirr Device Agent** icon to appear.

## 5. Change the phone to English

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **System**.
3. Tap **Languages & input**.
4. Tap **Languages**.
5. Tap **Add a language**.
6. Tap **English**.
7. Tap **United Kingdom** or **United States**.
8. Move **English** to the top of the list.
9. Return to **Settings**.
10. Tap **Date & time**.
11. Turn on **Set time automatically**.
12. Turn on **Set time zone automatically**.

## 6. Allow the applications to keep running

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Apps**.
3. Tap **OpenClaw**.
4. Tap **Battery**.
5. Tap **Unrestricted** or **No restrictions**.
6. Return to **Apps**.
7. Tap **Telebirr Device Agent**.
8. Tap **Battery**.
9. Tap **Unrestricted** or **No restrictions**.
10. Return to the home screen.

<!-- pagebreak -->

## 7. Connect OpenClaw

1. Open **OpenClaw**.
2. Tap **Connect**.
3. Tap **Setup Code**.
4. Scan the OpenClaw QR code or type the setup code supplied by the supervisor.
5. Tap **Pair** or **Submit**.
6. Wait for the supervisor to approve the phone.
7. Wait until the screen says **Connected** or **Online**.
8. Return to the home screen.

## 8. Give permissions to Telebirr Device Agent

1. Open **Telebirr Device Agent**.
2. When Android asks for SMS permission, tap **Allow**.
3. When Android asks for Phone permission, tap **Allow**.
4. When Android asks for notification permission, tap **Allow**.
5. Open **Settings**.
6. Tap **Accessibility**.
7. Tap **Downloaded apps**, **Installed services**, or **Accessibility services**.
8. Tap **Telebirr deterministic USSD control**.
9. Turn on **Use service**.
10. Tap **Allow**.
11. Return to **Telebirr Device Agent**.

<!-- pagebreak -->

## 9. Activate Telebirr Device Agent

1. Tap the **Device Gateway URL** field.
2. Type the Device Gateway URL supplied by the supervisor.
3. Tap the **One-time activation code** field.
4. Type the Telebirr Agent activation code supplied by the supervisor.
5. Tap **Activate**.
6. Wait until the screen shows **Activated**.
7. Tap **Confirm OpenClaw is paired**.
8. Wait for the next screen.

## 10. Enter the Telebirr PIN for SIM 1

1. Tap **SIM ICCID**.
2. Type the SIM 1 ICCID supplied by the supervisor.
3. Tap **Telebirr PIN (local only)**.
4. Type the Telebirr PIN for SIM 1.
5. Tap **Confirm Telebirr PIN (local only)**.
6. Type the same Telebirr PIN again.
7. Tap **Store local SIM PIN**.
8. Wait for the saved message.

<!-- pagebreak -->

## 11. Enter the Telebirr PIN for SIM 2

1. Tap **SIM ICCID**.
2. Delete the SIM 1 ICCID.
3. Type the SIM 2 ICCID supplied by the supervisor.
4. Tap **Telebirr PIN (local only)**.
5. Type the Telebirr PIN for SIM 2.
6. Tap **Confirm Telebirr PIN (local only)**.
7. Type the same Telebirr PIN again.
8. Tap **Store local SIM PIN**.
9. Wait for the saved message.

## 12. Finish the setup

1. Connect the charger.
2. Keep Wi-Fi turned on.
3. Open **Telebirr Device Agent**.
4. Send this message to the supervisor: `PHONE SETUP DONE`.
5. Leave the phone powered on and connected to the charger.
